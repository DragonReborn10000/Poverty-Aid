Why is this Important?

World hunger is a pressing issue affecting millions of people globally. It's a problem rooted in poverty, inequality, and food distribution. By addressing this issue, we can truly be a source of hope to the world.

The Problem

World Hunger Statistics

  • Less than 15% have access to adequate nutrition.
  • 15%-25% of the global population is undernourished.
  • 25%-35% suffer from food insecurity.
  • 35%-45% lack essential nutrients.
  • Over 45% are at risk of starvation.

The Impact of Hunger

Individual Consequences

Hunger has lasting effects on individuals, leading to malnutrition, poor health, and reduced productivity.

Economic and Social Consequences

World hunger can lead to economic instability and social unrest, affecting entire nations.

Global Consequences

Food scarcity in some regions can disrupt global food supply chains, impacting everyone.
